Post-Punk / Doom-Pop
BABELGAM
Bogotá duo blending post-punk dissonance with doomy, hypnotic pop textures — think Bauhaus filtered through a South American lens. One of the most distinctive sounds to emerge from the Colombian underground.

Psychedelic Cumbia
LA PAYARA
Cartagena-rooted psychedelic cumbia — traditional coastal rhythms run through a woozy, hallucinatory filter. La Payara's live sets are equal parts ritual and rave, pulling deep from Afro-Colombian tradition.

Experimental Cumbia / Avant-Garde
MERIDIAN BROTHERS
Bogotá's most singular experimental project, led by Eblis Álvarez since 1998 — fusing cumbia, vallenato, and electronic music into psychedelic, shape-shifting sounds unlike anything else. Featured on NPR Tiny Desk and KEXP.

LIKA NOVA
Cali-based project fusing indie pop-rock with the Pacific rhythms of Colombia's coastal south — marimba, cununos, and lush guitar woven into something entirely their own. Riding the wave of Colombia's international indie boom.
