Cover Story
Levitation Room have been chasing a sound since high school. Playing the Deer Lodge on May 1st, they're closer than ever.
Formed in Ojai around a mutual love of '60s garage rock played with a psychedelic twist, Julian Porte, Gabriel Fernandez, Jonathan Martin, and Kevin Perez have been quietly building one of the most compelling sounds on the Central Coast. We talked to them about growing up in the 805, the influence of the valley's landscape on their music, and what it means to stay DIY when labels come calling. Their Deer Lodge show May 1st is a must.
